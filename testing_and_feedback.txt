Testing and Feedback Steps:

1. Create a test plan that outlines the scenarios and test cases to be executed.
2. Conduct functional testing to ensure all features work as intended.
3. Perform usability testing to evaluate the app's user-friendliness.
4. Collect feedback from users through surveys, interviews, or feedback forms.
5. Analyze the feedback and identify common themes or areas for improvement.
6. Make necessary adjustments to the app based on the feedback.
7. Repeat the testing and feedback collection process to validate the changes.
8. Document the test results, feedback, and changes made for future reference.
