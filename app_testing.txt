Mobile App Testing Steps:

1. Prepare sample data for testing, including different natal charts and scenarios.
2. Test the app prototype by generating natal charts, retrieving energetic descriptions, and creating musical compositions.
3. Identify and document any bugs or issues encountered during testing.
4. Address and fix the identified bugs or issues.
5. Repeat the testing process with different sample data to ensure the app's functionality and accuracy.
6. Gather feedback from testers and make necessary improvements based on their input.
7. Conduct regression testing to ensure that bug fixes or new features do not introduce new issues.
8. Perform performance testing to assess the app's responsiveness and stability under different loads.
9. Conduct usability testing to evaluate the app's user-friendliness and ease of navigation.
10. Continuously review and update the app based on testing results and user feedback.
