Progress Review Steps:

1. Assess the completion status of each task, including research, UI design, mobile app development, API integration, algorithm design, and music feature implementation.
2. Identify any tasks that are incomplete or require further attention.
3. Evaluate the quality and effectiveness of the completed tasks.
4. Review the documentation and files created throughout the project to ensure they are accurate and up to date.
5. Consider any feedback or suggestions received from users or stakeholders.
6. Make a list of any adjustments or improvements that need to be made to ensure the successful completion of the project.
7. Prioritize the identified tasks and create a plan for addressing them.
8. Implement the necessary adjustments and improvements.
9. Continuously monitor and evaluate the progress of the project to ensure it stays on track.
